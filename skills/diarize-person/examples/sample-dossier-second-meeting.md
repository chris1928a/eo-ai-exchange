## What this dossier saves you

Before this skill: you'd open Gmail, search "Anna", scroll through 8 emails, open the calendar invite, find your old notes, try to remember what's the 1-pager promise was. ~10 minutes of pre-meeting fumbling.

With this skill: 30 seconds to read. You walk into the meeting knowing:
- The 1-pager you owe is overdue (act NOW or apologize at the start)
- She's analytical and references-driven, not relationship-driven
- Don't bring up the previous partner mess
- Three real questions you need to drive the conversation toward

That delta, 9.5 minutes per meeting × 4 meetings/day × 5 days = **3.2 hours per week** reclaimed JUST on prep, not counting the better meeting outcomes from being prepared.

---

## What's good about this output

1. **Last interaction flagged as today's commitment.** Bullet 1 of "Open threads" reminds you the 1-pager is overdue NOW.
2. **Sensitivity has evidence.** Not "she might not like that topic", explicit "mentioned in email 2026-04-21".
3. **Decision style cites a specific email.** Real prep, not horoscope.
4. **Open questions force the meeting agenda.** 3 specific questions, ordered by impact on outcome.
5. **Last-touch quote in original language.** You can pick up the thread mid-sentence.
6. **One page, exactly.** Fits on a phone screen.
