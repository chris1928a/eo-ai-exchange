Pattern-match against 3-5 of the best operators in your space. What would the smartest peer say to this?
A decision that contradicts 3 or more layers triggers a stop-and-think alert. The Twin refuses to push it through.

What’s under the hood

All corporate data.
Pulled live. Always current.

Script What it does
sales_bridge.py Sales-CRM live: opportunities, wons, lost, pipeline, lead pool, lost-reasons. On-demand snapshot with short cache.
delivery_bridge.py Delivery-CRM live: active mandates, companies, candidates or projects, ownership per team member.
financial_pull.py Live finance sheet pull: liquidity, headcount, monthly KPIs, drawdown trend, runway estimate.
pipeline_daily.py Daily snapshot + diff vs. yesterday + risk flags (stage stagnation, overdue tasks, pipeline drops).
stress_detector.py Pre-decision stress score across 4 trigger categories. Refuses to push a decision when red, suggests a pause template instead.
renewal_tracker.py Customer renewal-cycle from won-history + custom-field override. Heatmap of upcoming, in-window, overdue renewals.
All in Python. All in git. ~200 lines per pull. No vendor platform. No n8n, no make.com. Cron and Lambda for the schedules.
